# 📱 How to Access on Your Phone

This guide explains how to view and use your Safari RECEIPT website from your mobile device.

---

## Option 1: Using Your Local Wi-Fi (Quickest)

If you are at home or in the office and your phone is on the **same Wi-Fi network** as your computer, you can access the site directly.

### Steps:
1.  **Find your Computer's IP Address**:
    -   On your Windows computer, open the **Command Prompt** (search for `cmd`).
    -   Type `ipconfig` and press Enter.
    -   Look for "IPv4 Address" (e.g., `192.168.1.15`).
2.  **Open on Your Phone**:
    -   On your phone, open your browser (Chrome/Safari).
    -   Type the IP address followed by the port your live server is running on.
    -   **Example**: `http://192.168.1.15:5500`
    -   *(The port number is usually shown in VS Code when you click "Go Live" at the bottom right).*
